o	Antenna attached

•	SIM800L

o	SIM800L TX -> ESP32 RX1 (GPIO 16) (SIM TX -> ESP32 RX)
o	SIM800L RX -> ESP32 TX1 (GPIO 17) (SIM RX -> ESP32 TX)
o	SIM800L GND -> ESP32 GND (common ground)
o	Power: SIM800L needs a stable 4.0–4.2 V supply capable of 2A bursts. Do NOT power from ESP32 5V USB. Use a dedicated LiPo/regulator and add decoupling.

•	Power for ESP32: 5V USB adapter or appropriate regulated supply.
